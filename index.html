Federal Gang & Narcotics Agency (FGNA)

The Federal Gang & Narcotics Agency (FGNA) is a premier federal law enforcement agency dedicated to combating violent criminal organizations involved in narcotics trafficking and organized crime. FGNA focuses on dismantling the operations of the Madrazo Cartel (MC), the Ruskova Federation (RF)—a Russian organized crime syndicate—and the street gangs Prophet$ and the Ballas.

Mission Overview

FGNA’s mission is to safeguard communities and uphold public safety by disrupting and dismantling criminal networks engaged in drug trafficking, violent crime, and territorial control. Through intelligence-driven enforcement and coordinated multi-jurisdictional operations, FGNA targets criminal leadership, disrupts illicit supply chains, and weakens street-level organizations.

Key Operational Focus

  • Madrazo Cartel (MC): A powerful narcotics cartel dominating international drug trafficking routes and employing violence and intimidation to maintain control.
  • Ruskova Federation (RF): A sophisticated Russian organized crime group engaged in narcotics distribution, arms trafficking, and complex criminal enterprises.
  • Prophet$ and the Ballas: Notorious street gangs controlling key urban territories, deeply involved in drug trafficking, racketeering, and violent crime.
